Who are F.A.A.L?

Fathers Against Arch Linux is an organisation that has grown out of necessity. We are a collective that have lost our children to the ways of arch linux.

Parents will teach their children about the dangers of crossing the road without looking both ways, speaking to strangers and drugs. Far too few parents are aware of what arch linux is, let alone the dangers of it.

We have made it our mission to make sure no children are lost to the dangers of Arch Linux ever again.
